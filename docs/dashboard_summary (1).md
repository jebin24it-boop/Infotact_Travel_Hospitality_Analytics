## **Dashboard Summary**

This dashboard was developed using Power BI to analyze hotel reservation data. It provides interactive insights into booking trends, cancellations, room pricing, and customer behavior through KPIs, charts, and slicers. 

## **1\. Executive Overview**

This page gives the overall summary of hotel reservations. It includes KPIs such as Total Bookings, Cancelled Bookings, Cancellation Rate, Average Room Price, Average Lead Time, and Repeat Guest Rate. It also shows booking trends by month and bookings by market segment.

## **2\. Cancellation Analysis**

This page analyzes cancellation patterns. It shows cancellations based on lead time, room type, meal plan, and market segment. This helps identify which factors are linked with higher cancellations.

## **3\. Revenue & Pricing Analysis**

This page focuses on room pricing. It shows Average Room Price, Average Price for Cancelled Bookings, and Average Price for Not Cancelled Bookings. It also compares pricing by month, room type, and cancellation status.

## 

## **4\. Customer Insights**

This page explains customer behavior. It shows Total Guests, Repeat Guests, New Guests, and Repeat Guest Rate. It also includes customer segments, repeat vs new guests, and guest composition analysis.

## **Conclusion**

The completed dashboard provides an interactive and comprehensive analysis of hotel reservation data. It enables users to monitor key performance indicators, identify cancellation trends, analyze pricing patterns, and understand customer behavior, supporting better business decision-making.
